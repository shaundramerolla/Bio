Travels

My love of traveling began when I spent a semester abroad in Rome. Since then I road-tripped Northern California, stayed in a tree house in Oregon, and enjoyed my local New England states. My most recent trip was to Richmond, Virgina. This gallery is a snapshot of some of my most favorite memories of the places I have traveled.
