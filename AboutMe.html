Introduction

Born and raised in Massachusetts, I grew up in a small town northwest of Boston, Shirley, Massachusetts, population 7000. I attended the University of Rhode Island, where I earned a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration. I spent my first few years in the workforce in IT Deployment and Marketing at a fortune 100 company in Boston's Metrowest. I currently work as an Analyst, managing mobile app and delivery content for the Dunkin' and Baskin-Robbins menu.

In my free time, I work on fixing up an old Dutch Colonial (circa 1910) my husband and I bought two years ago. We now live in Attleboro with our dog named Sadie. She's a (very spoiled) rescue from Tennessee. When I find free time I enjoy cooking, traveling, and listening to music.
