One of my own favorite recipes is something I call Mole Chicken:

Ingredients

  • 4 Whole Chicken Legs (with skin)
  • 3 Mole Peppers, chopped
  • 1 Onion, sliced
  • 2 Cups Chicken Stock
  • about 1 TBSP of fresh Cilantro, chopped
  • Kosher Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Place chicken in Dutch oven
  2. Add Salt and Pepper to taste
  3. Place Dutch oven in oven, uncovered
  4. Broil on high until chicken skin is brown and bubbled
  5. Remove from oven
  6. Add the chicken stock, peppers, onions, and cilantro
  7. Cover and return to oven
  8. Bake on 375 degrees for 1 hour

Shred chicken, and serve with corn tortillas or quinoa.

Mole peppers are hotter than bell, but milder than jalapeno. You can play around with different types of peppers to make the dish as spicy as you like.
