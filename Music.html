Music

Music has always been a big part of my life. I spent some time in the middle school band, learning trumpet, drums, and flute. Instruments never stuck with me, but I spent four years in my high school chorus, and still enjoy singing to my dashboard. I love country, alternative, 90's rock, and especially classic rock. Most recently, I was lucky enough to see Fleetwood Mac in Boston. Over the past decade, I've been able to see artists like Kenny Chesney, Zac Brown Band, the Dixie Chicks, along with some of my other favorite artists featured on this page.
